नेपाली खसीले स्थानीय बाख्रापालनमार्फत ताजा र जैविक खसी र मासु प्रदान गर्छ। हाम्रो लक्ष्य स्थानीय किसानलाई समर्थन गर्दै ग्रामीण अर्थतन्त्रलाई बलियो बनाउनु हो। बजारमा उच्च माग भएकाले हाम्रो वैज्ञानिक व्यवस्थापन र गुणस्तरीय उत्पादनले यो व्यवसायलाई अझ आकर्षक बनाउँछ।

About Us

Nepali Goat, Nepali Taste — Now Online!


Goat farming is a vital part of Nepalese agriculture. Goat and buck rearing has been a reliable income source in rural areas. We collaborate directly with local farmers to bring fresh, healthy, and high-quality goat meat to your doorstep. We are a digital initiative bridging the gap between producers and consumers. Our goal is to bring the products of hardworking livestock farmers from Nepal's rural areas directly to the market. We believe that respecting local production makes the nation self-reliant.
